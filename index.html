Guide

Your crypto learning roadmap

Step 1: Understand the basics

Before buying anything, learn what blockchain actually is and why Bitcoin was created. Understanding the underlying technology helps you evaluate every other project and avoid scams.

Step 2: Set up a secure wallet

Your wallet is your bank. Learn the difference between custodial and non-custodial wallets, hot and cold storage, and why your seed phrase is the most important thing you'll ever write down.

Step 3: Explore beyond Bitcoin

Once you understand the fundamentals, explore DeFi protocols, smart contract platforms, and staking. Each topic builds on the last, and rushing ahead without the basics is how people lose money.

Step 4: Practice with small amounts

Start small. Use testnets to practice transactions, swap tiny amounts on a DEX, and stake a minimal position. Real experience teaches more than any guide.
